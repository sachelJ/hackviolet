Introduction

This page will teach you about cashing in your social security benefits and how to plan for your future in a way that maximizes your social security benefits.

4 Main Types of Investing

  • Growth Investing: selecting promising companies that are expected to experience large growth - even if stock prices are higher
  • Value Investing-matching investments with stock market behavior - buying stock at a low point and selling when prices increase
  • Quality Investing-choosing credible companies that have proved theirselves by being very stable and maybe even predictable to a point due to their high standards held.
  • Index Investing-this is the most similar to passive investing where investments are coordinated to specific markets

How to Start Investing

  1. Decide how much money you are willing to invest and decide on an investing strategy (conservative or risky)
  2. Build your portfolio by researching companies and deciding what route you are going to take (stocks,bonds, hig interest accounts)
  3. Pick a platform to invest in- Robinhood is a great starter platform for this
  4. Buy your stocks and track your progress as the market grows/falls
