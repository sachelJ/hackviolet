Introduction

Saving for retirement is an important step in ensuring a comfortable future. Starting early, like in college, can provide a significant advantage. This page will explain the benefits of starting a 401k plan in college and how to get started.

Benefits of Starting Early

  • Compound Interest: The earlier you start saving, the more time your money has to grow through compound interest.
  • Lower Contributions: The earlier you start saving, the lower the amount you will need to contribute each month to reach your retirement goals.
  • Employer Matching: Some employers match a portion of the contributions you make to your 401k, effectively increasing your savings rate.

How to Start a 401k

  1. Research plans offered by your employer.
  2. Choose a plan and enroll in it.
  3. Set up automatic contributions from your paycheck to your 401k plan.
  4. Track your progress and adjust your contributions as needed.
